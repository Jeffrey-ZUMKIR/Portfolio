Cyber Drunk

Name: Cyber Drunk

Type: Rhythm, infiltration

Plateform: Construct 2

Player: 1

Pitch

On browser, become a thief and collect the most bottle of alcohol in this cyberpunk world by using the music to hide your footsteps from the bouncers.


Context

"Cyber Drunk" is a project created during the Mix and Game Jam 2020 in 48 hours, the theme was mixing to kind of game. It was my first my first jam and I took the role of the graphic designer.


Intention

  • Train my pixel art skills.
  • Realize all the sprites required in a short period of time:
    • The main character and his animations
    • 9 NPCs with their animation
    • A tilemap for the background
    • A tilemap for the decoration
    • Various objects
