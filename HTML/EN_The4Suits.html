The 4 Suits

Name: The 4 Suits

Type: Plateformer

Plateform: Unreal Engine 4

Player: 1

Pitch

On PC, explore worlds made around the 4 suits of cards and start looking for every card of the deck to obtain the best score.


Contexte

"The 4 Suits" is a project created during an exam, our instructions were to make a game on Unreal Engine in blueprint in 1 week and we needed 4 levels based a related theme, I choosed the 4 suits of cards.


Intention

  • Realize a game in blueprint.
  • Use my knowledges of blueprint that I got with the blueprint tutorial on "gamedev.tv".
  • Plan my time to realize every mechanics and the level design.
