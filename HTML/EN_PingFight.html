Ping Fight

Name: Ping Fight

Type: Sport, fight

Plateform: Construct 3

Player: 2-4

Pitch

In multiplayer on PC in local network, become a penguin and face your friends on an ice hockey field by shooting a puck to hit your opponents. Score most of the round to win the game.


Context

"Ping Fight" is a project created during a Game Jam in Ludus Academie in 24 hours, the theme was "ice rink". We set ourselves as a base the ice hockey and I was one of the programmers.

For this jam, we had the possibility to do it face-to-face so we wanted to use this opportunity to make a multiplayer game. The objective was to make a fun game where you are out of control due to the speed of the puck.


Intention

  • Work in collaboration with a second programmer.
  • Realize a game where the flow is omnipresent.
  • Realize a game in a short period of time.
  • Understand the mechanics for multiplayer game.
