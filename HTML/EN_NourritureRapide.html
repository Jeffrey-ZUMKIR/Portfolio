Nourriture Rapide

Name: Nourriture Rapide

Type: Beat them-all

Plateform: Construct 3

Player: 1

Pitch

On browser, become a young cook and defend your restaurant against vegetables until the end of the allotted time.


Context

"Nourriture Rapide" is a project created for the McGame Jam 2021 in 48 hours, the theme was "unexpected consequence". For this project I took the role of the game designer and project manager. I wanted to train my team management skills. My role was to set up a list of the things that needed to be done and define the priorities.

We wanted to submit a dynamic game, so we decided to make a "beat them-all". To diversify the gameplay, we implemented two types of attacks. The first one is a dash and the second one is a special capacitate depending on which object you are carrying. To go along the jam's theme, our unexpected consequence is our final boss. When you kill enemies, your restaurant is getting dirty and the amount of dirt in restaurant become the life of the final boss.


Intention

  • Manage a team and a project.
  • Respect the thme of the jam.
  • Take into account the capabilities of my partners.
  • Facilitate communication between the graphic designer and the programmer.
